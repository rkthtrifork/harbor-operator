# Technical Debt

This register is for material shortcomings that are deliberately left in the current implementation. It is not a list of possible enhancements, minor cleanup, or planned product features; those belong in the backlog or the issue tracker.

For each item, record:

- the observable shortcoming and affected area
- why it remains unresolved
- operational, compatibility, or maintenance consequences
- the condition or decision that would justify resolving it
- a tracking issue when one exists

Remove entries when they are resolved or no longer accurate. There is no recorded technical debt at present.
